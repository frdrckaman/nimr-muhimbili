Corporate Services Department

Activities Performed by Corporate Services Department

  • 01. To advise the Centre Management on various financial matters
  • 02. The department facilitates day to day Centre operations by planning, controlling, and organizing all Centre financial and accounting operations.
  • 03. Lead the Centre budgeting process and budget control.
  • 04. Preparing general financial periodic and final reports.
  • 05. Support other departments in short term activity planning, especially budget preparation and payments.
  • 06. Responsible for maintenance of proper books of accounts and keeping of all records pertaining to income and expenditure as well as inventory of the Institute.
  • 07. Ensure that adequate provisions exist for the safe custody of Institute’s money, securities and accountable documents.
  • 08. Manage Centre’s Income and revenue sources and its day to day records
